I watched [Pakistan's Hidden Shame](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NMp2wm0VMUs) today, a film about the exploitation of street children (mainly boys) in Pakistan. The film's site is [here](https://www.pakistanshiddenshame.org/single-post/2016/03/30/This-is-the-title-of-your-first-post).

I felt incredibly helpless and depressed during and completing the film. And there is nothing I can do. As a gay man, it especially disgusts me that what should be consensual and celebrated is being perverted this way.

My thoughts wandered onto "humanity's state of affairs", if you will. We live in one of the most prosperous and peaceful time periods. And still, China is aborting girls at rates that make Stalin & Hitler seem like children. Boys sell themselves in Pakistan and Afghanistan due to sexual repression, gender discrimination, and poverty. Latin America remains in the thrall of dictators, military control, drugs, and gang violence (in no small part thanks to US efforts). America's democracy has fallen to religious insanity and conspiracy theories (with the population not far behind (though the 2020 election may reverse this)). Europe is also coming under the same far-right influences. The Middle East is becoming increasingly violent. And in Northern Africa (is that the term?) FGM is common. I think I got nearly the whole world there.

I see know why people turn to religion, regardless of supporting evidence. We want to believe that we are being watched over, the everyone gets what's coming (good or bad). Religion gives us (false) hope.
